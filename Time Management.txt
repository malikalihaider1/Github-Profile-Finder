EFECTIVE TIME MANAGEMENT TIPS

1)SET CLEAR GOALS:
Define your objectives to stay focused on what truly matters.
2)PRIORITIZE TASKS:
Rank tasks by importance and urgency to tackle high-priority items first.
3)NO DISTRACTIONS:
Disable notifications and establish a tranquil work environment.
4)SET TIME LIMITS:
Establish time constraints for tasks to mitigate the risk of overcommitment to a single activity.
5)LEARN TO SAY NO:
Politely decline additional commitments that may overload your schedule.
5)TO DO LIST:
You can also use To Do List.
